Lasagna

Description

This classic Italian lasagna recipe is rich, cheesy, and satisfying. It layers sheets of pasta with a hearty meat sauce and a creamy cheese blend for the ultimate comfort dish.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic and onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles.
  2. Cook beef with garlic and onion, then add tomato sauce.
  3. Layer sauce, noodles, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and top with mozzarella and Parmesan.
  5. Bake at 375Â°F for 45 minutes.
  6. Let it rest before serving.
