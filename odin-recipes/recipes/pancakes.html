Pancakes

Description

These fluffy pancakes are perfect for breakfast or brunch. Serve with maple syrup, fresh fruits, or a pat of butter for a delicious start to your day.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Egg
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. In another bowl, mix milk, egg, and vanilla.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then add melted butter.
  4. Heat a pan and pour batter into circles.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  6. Serve warm with toppings of choice.
