Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a quick and delicious way to use up leftover rice. It combines fluffy rice with savory soy sauce, vegetables, and proteins for a tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • Cooked rice
  • Eggs
  • Vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • Soy sauce
  • Garlic and onions
  • Sesame oil
  • Cooked chicken or shrimp (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and scramble eggs. Set aside.
  2. Cook garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add vegetables and cook until tender.
  4. Stir in rice and mix thoroughly.
  5. Add soy sauce and cooked eggs (and meat if using).
  6. Stir-fry for a few more minutes and serve hot.
